ALUMNI MEET

About

To continue our everlasting ties with our alumnus we here at Army Institute of Technology conduct Alumni Meet once in every three year. Our ideology is to motivate our current students to do much better in the future by the suggestions and notable examples displayed by our former students. This distinguished program help the students to view positive and negative aspects of life in a different light. Different clubs of Army Institute of Technology welcome our previous students with a joyful heart by organising different games and events like Robo war and quirky ideas.

About

The Cultural Fest of AIT is one of the most awaited events in Pune. With 4000-5000 students in the college for 3 days with passion running down their veins and brains going crazy with ideas and a hunger to perform and showcase all that they have, Amethyst becomes the talk of the town. With events in all domains and performances from prominent bands like AFTER ACOUSTICS, CHEHRE, ABRAXAS and MH43. Amethyst becomes a not to be missed affair in the student community.

INNERVE

About

INNERVE is one of the most macro-scale hackathon annually organized by the OSS Club of Army Institute of Technology, Pune for students, developers, and tech enthusiasts to explore their expertise in developing innovative products for society. The Idea Pitching round is an opportunity to exhibit your real-time, innovative, problem-solving ideas and concepts. In the Building Blocks round sessions from various industry expert take place , which marks the commencement of celebrated 24 hours Hackathon. In this round, they envisage you to practically use your concepts and propound them with developing innovative products.
